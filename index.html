Cowbird is a storytelling site. Sometimes I feel that the art of storytelling has disappeared in the digital age. Cowbird allows people to share stories more globally. I sometimes share stories.

Running and cycling has been a big part of my life for a long time (most of my life). A lot of times I can't meet up with friends to run or ride. Strava has really helped me share fitness activities with friends. Check me out.

Projects

Mail Victory

Responsive email dev

Mail Victory was a blog I started to talk about email development after I built a responsive email templating system. I built modular html bits so that templating bulletproof responsive emails was fairly simple. I've started my documentation here.

I have to say, I really like cats. After not being able to have a cat I started to 'fork' cat pictures so that I could see more cats during the day. It's basic but fun and I have quite a few cute cat pics there.
