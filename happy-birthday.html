Happy Birthday, a project to say happy birthday

May 5, 2014

A year ago I was not able to be with one of my dear friends on their birthday so I made this.

This being posed images of myself to make each letter for the word Happy Birthday.

I posted the images on Instagram in reverse order so that my friends could slowly get the message that I had posed for them.
